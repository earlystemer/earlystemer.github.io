About earlySTEMer

earlySTEMer is an online platform where your kids can learn STEM skills with little or no help from the convenience of your home

Here at earlySTEMer, kids have access to :

  • Learn STEM skills such as Coding, Web design, and Robotics completely online
  • Work on and submit projects that will be graded
  • Have live one-on-one tutoring sessions when they need help
